Clarity. Precision. Academic integrity.

CLEARLINE provides professional proofreading, editing, and academic refinement services for tertiary-level students. We work carefully with your academic writing to improve clarity, structure, language accuracy, and logical flow — while preserving your original meaning, argument, and academic voice.

Our aim is simple: to help you present your work at its best standard, clearly, ethically, and professionally.

What We Do

Proofreading
Correction of grammar, spelling, punctuation, typing, and basic formatting errors.

Editing
Language and structural editing to improve clarity, coherence, academic tone, and overall readability.

Refinement
Careful, in-depth refinement of long assignments, research reports, mini-dissertations, dissertations, and theses at Honours, Master’s, and Doctoral level – including correct in-text referencing and construction of a List of Sources.

Where appropriate, we also provide:

  • Consistency checks (terminology, headings, tables, figures)
  • Referencing and citation style alignment (Harvard, Chicago, APA etc.)
  • Language support for students writing in English as an additional language
