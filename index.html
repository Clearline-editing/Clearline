Professional Proofreading, Editing & Academic Refinement

Clarity. Precision. Academic integrity.

CLEARLINE provides professional proofreading, editing, and academic refinement services for tertiary-level students. We work carefully with your academic writing to improve clarity, structure, language accuracy, and logical flow — while preserving your original meaning, argument, and academic voice.

Our aim is simple: to help you present your work at its best standard, clearly, ethically, and professionally.

What We Do

  • Proofreading: Correction of grammar, spelling, punctuation, typing, and basic formatting errors.
  • Editing: Language and structural editing to improve clarity, coherence, academic tone, and overall readability.
  • Refinement: In-depth refinement of assignments, research reports, dissertations, and theses, including referencing and structure.

Additional support includes consistency checks, referencing alignment (Harvard, APA, Chicago), and language support for non-native English speakers.

Who CLEARLINE Supports

  • Undergraduate students
  • Postgraduate students
  • Honours, Master’s, and PhD candidates
  • Professionals preparing academic or research documents
  • Students writing in English as an additional language

How It Works

  1. Submission & Enquiry: Send your document with details (level, deadline, referencing style).
  2. Assessment & Quotation: CLEARLINE reviews your work and provides a quote.
  3. Editing Process: Careful, ethical editing using tracked changes.
  4. Delivery: Edited document returned with optional feedback.

Pricing & Turnaround

Fees depend on word count, academic level, and depth of editing. Pricing may be per-word, per-hour, or project-based.

Turnaround: Agreed in advance based on length, complexity, and workload. Urgent work accommodated where possible.

Academic Integrity

CLEARLINE adheres strictly to academic integrity principles. We do not write or alter intellectual ownership. All work remains the student’s own.

Every document is handled with strict confidentiality and professional care. CLEARLINE understands academic pressure and is committed to helping you present clear, high-quality work.
